Khaida

Al Qaida Al Nooraniya is a book for beginners to learn Quranic Arabic, is used to teach children how to read the Quran. This book was compiled by Sheikh Noor Muhammad Haqqani.
The book teaches the correct pronunciation of the Arabic alphabets, using passages from the Quran. It begins with the Arabic alphabets, showing how they are connected.
